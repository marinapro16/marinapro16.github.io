SOCIAL CAMPAIGN

PTSD From School Shootings

I created this social campaign in my Typography II class, and it addresses the PTSD and long-term effects experienced by students who survive school shootings. “Partnering” with the Sandy Hook Promise, the campaign aims to raise awareness and advocate for change. The campaign slogan, "NEVER AGAIN", features the word "never" crossed out, symbolizing the tragic reality that despite our repeated vows, such incidents continue to occur. As someone who has personally endured a school shooting and suffers from PTSD, creating this campaign holds a deep meaning for myself.
