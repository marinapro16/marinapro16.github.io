SIX-WORD MEMOIR

Accordian Style Project Representing Myself

This project was made in my beginner Design Principles class. I created a project centered around a six-word memoir, selecting words that best describe myself: Loving, Creative, California, Girl, Nature, and Clever. Initially, I experimented with collages using magazines and photographs to develop multiple studies for each word.

After finalizing the collages and color palette, I conducted typography studies to determine the best placement of text. Once the final typography was chosen, I assembled all my designs on thick boards, crafting a foldable accordion-style presentation.
