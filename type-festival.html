TYPE FESTIVAL

The Future Of Type Is Evolving

For this project in my Graphic Design III class, I conceptualized and created a type festival, focusing on the theme "The Future of Type is Evolving." The centerpiece of my work is a video introduction for the festival, where I chose to explore the evolution of typography from traditional methods, such as ink on paper and typewriters, to contemporary techniques involving complex computer systems, 3D type, and motion. To create this project, I used Adobe After Effects and Cinema 4D, synchronizing visual changes with musical beats to enhance the viewer's experience. The choice of music and color palette was inspired by the title sequence of the film "Leave the World Behind."
