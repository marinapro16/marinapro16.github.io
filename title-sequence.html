TITLE SEQUENCE

Because of Winn-Dixie

In my Graphic Design III class, I created a title sequence for Because of Winn-Dixie, building on a previous book cover project. After rewatching the film to deepen my understanding of its themes and symbolism, I explored various visual concepts and produced teaser clips.

I ultimately chose my “clothesline” concept to highlight community and connection, using After Effects to bring it to life. The sequence features silhouettes of children with embedded videos to evoke nostalgia and joy, accompanied by a guitar soundtrack that enhances the sentimental tone.
