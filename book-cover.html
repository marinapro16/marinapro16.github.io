BOOK COVERS

Because of Winn-Dixie

This project was for my Graphic Design I class. To start, I researched "Because of Winn-Dixie" by studying the book and movie, focusing on themes like connection, community, love, and friendship. I conducted shape, type, and collage studies to explore these themes deeply.

My designs aim to be playful, visually beautiful, and reflective of the book's essence. The cover highlighted above emphasizes community and love, featuring a dog bowl filled with cutout houses. This symbolizes how Winn-Dixie, the stray dog adopted by the protagonist India Opal, brings the community together.

India Opal, a young girl who moves to a new town, finds friendship and belonging through her bond with Winn-Dixie, illustrating the dog's impact on fostering connections and unity within the town.
